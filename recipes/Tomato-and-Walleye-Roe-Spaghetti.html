Tomato and Walleye Roe Spaghetti

Description:

Tomato and Walleye Roe Spaghetti, With the meat on bone make yourself a pirate lunch box!

Ingredients For 2 Serves:

  • 180g spaghetti
  • 2000mL water
  • 1, (10x10cm) sheet kombu seaweed
  • 10g salt
sauce:
  • 60g walleye roe
  • 3-4 tbsp diced tomato
  • 10g butter
  • finely chopped nori (as needed)

How To Cook:

  1. Put kombu, salt and water in a pot and heat. Take the walleye roe out of the thin skin. Put butter, tomato and walleye roe in a bowl.
  2. Follow the directions on the spaghetti bag. When finished, dress with the ingredients from step one. If you would like to weaken the flavor, add a little water (from the spaghetti). Top with nori.
